about f1

Formula 1 (F1) is the world’s most prestigious and thrilling motorsport. It features ultra-fast, high-tech cars racing at speeds over 350 km/h on legendary tracks across the globe. Governed by the FIA (Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile), F1 brings together the best drivers, cutting-edge technology, and intense team strategies in a battle for glory.

F1 cars are not ordinary vehicles. They’re open-wheel, single-seater machines powered by hybrid V6 turbo engines and built with extreme aerodynamics for maximum performance. Features like DRS (Drag Reduction System) and advanced tires allow them to corner and accelerate like no other race cars.
